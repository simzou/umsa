Membership


What are the benefits of joining UMSA?

Members of UMSA will have access to:

  • Our academic network, meeting and interacting with fellow math majors and professors outside of lectures.
  • Fun social events, such as mathematical scavenger hunts, quarterly dinners and BBQs.
  • Interesting research lectures by esteemed faculty at UCLA.
  • Leadership opportunities, such as becoming a Mentor or an UMSA Officer.
  • Academic programs, such as Matlab and LaTex tutorials and Graduate Student and REU panels.
  • Listing contact information on the public tutoring page.
  • Our mentorship program. Have an experienced mentor guide you through being a Math major at UCLA.
  • Annual members also receive our new UMSA CLUB TSHIRT!


How do I join UMSA?

To join, please fill this form out and talk to an officer at the next gathering to pay dues.

Annual membership dues are $30 but if you register by Third Week of the Fall Quarter, we will apply a $5 discount, totaling to $25 for the entire year
